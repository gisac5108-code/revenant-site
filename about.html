REVENANT

When a boy is pushed to the edge of a world ruled by darkness and rebirth, he learns a truth most stories avoid.

In life, no hero is guaranteed to save you. Anyone can betray you. And sometimes, to survive, you must become the villain of the story.

Revenant is a dark fantasy about standing alone, confronting who you are, and rising when the world demands you break.

Some stories are about being chosen.
This one is about choosing yourself.
